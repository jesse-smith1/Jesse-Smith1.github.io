What is the difference between Margin, Border, and Padding?

The Margin is the outskirts of the element

The "Border" borders the element.

The Padding. The space between the border (whether visible or not) and the content.

Think of a a box

Your margin is the distance you want your box to be (or even overlapping) another “box” (element). Your border is what’s “bordering” your element (in this case it would be your actual box) and the contents that it is holding. (Like a bag of chips that has all that extra air in the packet. The padding is the air).
