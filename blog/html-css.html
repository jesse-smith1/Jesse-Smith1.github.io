What is the difference between Margin, Border, and Padding?

The Margin is the outskirts of the element
The "Border" borders the element.
The Padding is the space between the border (whether visible or not) and the content.

Think of a box

Your margin is the distance you want your box to be (or even overlapping) another “box” (element).


Here is a picture of what a margin code may look like.


This tells you that you want your element to be (in this example) 20 pixels away from another element on the Top, right, bottom and left side of this element.


Your border is whats “bordering” your element (in this case it would be your actual box)
And the padding is the distance between the border and your content
